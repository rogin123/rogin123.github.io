About Me

I'm a research assistant at the Urban Institute in the Metropolitan Housing and Communities Policy center. My research focuses on flood resilience, transportation equity, and disaster preparedness. I graduated from Northwestern University 2020 (a famously fantastic time to be finishing college) where I studied environmental science, data science, and Middle Eastern studies.

When I'm not typing away at my computer for work, I like to type away at my computer futzing around with data projects, read about how the internet is influencing society, bike, hike, and be a gen Z about town in DC.
